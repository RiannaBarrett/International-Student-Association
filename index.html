International Student Association

The International Student Association's main objectives are to improve the friendship, interaction, and understanding of international students as well as to advance their well-being on campus. Students who were not born in the US will be able to share aspects of their unique and incredibly varied cultural backgrounds with the YCP community on a cultural,academic, and social level through the International Student Association.
The International Student Association works to promote international students on campus and to inform those in the community.
